Lights Out



How To Play


Turn the lights out!



Starting 3x3 Board:


Clicking on a square will toggle the light for that, and surrounding squares:


On a 3x3 board, click each of the corners and then the middle square:


And you win!


Unlike the original iteration of this game, clicking on an “OFF” square does nothing.

A 3x3 board is a gimme.

4x4 is still pretty easy.

5x5 is tough.

I have yet to complete a 6x6 board.
